# FUTURE_DS_02 – Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

Customer churn analysis helps businesses understand why customers stop using a service.

In this project, customer subscription data was analyzed to identify churn patterns, customer retention behavior, and potential factors influencing customer loss.

The objective is to provide insights that can help businesses improve customer retention and reduce churn.

---

## Dataset

Dataset used: Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset contains customer information such as:

- Customer demographics
- Subscription services
- Contract type
- Monthly charges
- Tenure
- Payment method
- Churn status

## Exploratory Data Analysis

### Churn Rate Analysis
Calculated the percentage of customers who left the service.

### Contract Type vs Churn
Compared churn behavior across different contract types.

### Monthly Charges Analysis
Analyzed whether higher charges influence churn.

### Tenure Analysis
Observed how long customers typically stay with the service.

### Payment Method Analysis
Studied how different payment methods relate to churn.

---

## Key Insights

• Customers with month-to-month contracts show higher churn rates.

• Customers with longer tenure are less likely to churn.

• Higher monthly charges tend to correlate with higher churn.

• Certain payment methods are associated with increased churn.

---

## Business Recommendations

• Encourage customers to move to longer-term contracts.

• Provide loyalty incentives for long-term users.

• Offer discounts or bundles to customers with high monthly charges.

• Improve customer engagement for new customers during early tenure.
